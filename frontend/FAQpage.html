Frequently Asked Questions

What are Student Center hours?
The Student Center is open Mon-Fri, 9 a.m.-5 p.m.
How do I book a room?
Rooms can be booked through our online Room Booking page, or you request a room at the front desk insinde the Student Center.
Can student organizations host events here?
Yes, registered student organizations can host events in designated areas. Approval from Student Center office is required before booking.
Is food allowed in study areas?
Bottled drinks and snacks are permitted in the study areas, we just ask that you please keep these areas clean to ensure a relaxing, comfortable environment.
Are there printing services available?
There are printers in the study areas that are free for students to use. Please only print what you need to ensure others have access to printer resources if they need it.
